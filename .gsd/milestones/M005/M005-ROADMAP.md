# M005: 

## Vision
Transform ThreadMoat from a dashboard of charts into an intelligence workbench where users explore data, shortlist companies, and generate expert-level narrative reports with AI-powered analysis.

## Slice Overview
| ID | Slice | Risk | Depends | Done | After this |
|----|-------|------|---------|------|------------|
| S01 | Filter Workflow Onboarding | low | — | ✅ | New user sees a brief tooltip or guide explaining the filter→chart workflow on first visit. |
| S02 | AI Narrative Engine for Report Generator | high | — | ✅ | Report generator produces AI-generated sections: Impressions, Conclusions, Beware, Overlooked Opportunities for selected companies. |
| S03 | Company Shortlist / Workspace | medium | — | ✅ | User clicks companies across any chart to add them to a shortlist. Shortlisted companies are highlighted across all charts and available in the report builder. |
| S04 | Custom Report Builder | high | S02, S03 | ✅ | User selects filters, picks companies, chooses charts, clicks Generate — gets a narrative PDF/document with all selections and AI analysis. |
